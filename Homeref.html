CD Projekt Red's homepage is a visually striking and user-friendly portal into the world of one of gaming's most celebrated developers, known for The Witcher series and Cyberpunk 2077. The site showcases their latest games with bold imagery, trailers, and updates while providing a news feed with announcements and behind-the-scenes insights. Visitors can explore the studio's mission, community highlights, and career opportunities, as well as shop for official merchandise. With its sleek design and comprehensive content, the homepage reflects CD Projekt Red's dedication to storytelling, innovation, and engaging with their passionate fan base.
